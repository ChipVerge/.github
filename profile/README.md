# ChipVerge — RTL Design & Verification

Practical RTL, verification, and EDA tooling focused on producing review-ready artifacts and reproducible verification flows.

ChipVerge publishes reference hardware, verification environments, and automation that demonstrate disciplined design and verification practices. Our materials emphasize lint-clean SystemVerilog, CDC awareness, and coverage-driven verification to help engineers build more reliable silicon.

---

## Our Open Source Mission

ChipVerge maintains open-source hardware, verification, and tooling resources on GitHub. Our goals are to improve silicon quality, accelerate engineering education, and make robust verification practices broadly accessible.

What we publish:
- Reference RTL modules and IP examples
- UVM environments, testbenches, and verification utilities
- CDC-safe patterns, lint-rule examples, and SVA checks
- EDA automation scripts and CI recipes (Python, Tcl)
- Tutorials, workshop materials, and hands-on training labs

Where appropriate, artifacts are published under permissive open-source licenses to encourage reuse and collaboration. These projects are developed independently of client engagements and are shared to support education, research, and the engineering community. See individual repositories for license details.
