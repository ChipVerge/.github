What We Offer

Current Services

Deep specialization in the frontend design phase, ensuring correct-by-construction RTL.

Available Now

Frontend RTL Design

We design synthesizable RTL in SystemVerilog/VHDL for custom IPs, subsystems, and SoC blocks. Our deliverables are lint-clean, CDC-safe, and synthesis-ready.

  • SystemVerilog & VHDL
  • Micro-architecture definition
  • Lint & CDC analysis
  • Synthesis handoff ready
Available Now

RTL Verification

Rigorous functional verification using UVM-based testbenches, constrained-random stimulus, coverage closure, and formal property checking.

  • UVM testbench development
  • Constrained-random testing
  • Functional coverage closure
  • Formal / SVA-based checks
