Personal Story

The Journey

Born and raised in Dhaka, Bangladesh, I was always drawn to the world of electronics. The moment that truly captured my imagination was learning that billions of MOSFETs — each doing nothing more than switching on and off — could be embedded into a single chip and collectively produce extraordinary computational power. That insight sent me down a path I haven't looked back from.

In 2022, I completed my Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from American International University Bangladesh (AIUB). Throughout my studies, I felt a genuine pull toward electronic devices and digital design. A Verilog course during my degree gave me my first hands-on taste of hardware description languages, and it sparked everything that followed.

During my final semester, I was offered a trainee position at a VLSI engineering firm in Bangladesh — an opportunity I grabbed without hesitation. Over six months of intensive training under experienced professionals, I absorbed the fundamentals of the field while simultaneously beginning work for a US-based client. That dual track — learning and delivering — shaped how I approach every project today.

After completing my training, I joined as a Junior Design Verification Engineer and continued expanding my scope. My early work centred on AXI4 custom IP verification using both class-based SystemVerilog and UVM. I also developed a testbench for a RISC-V floating point unit using Python cocotb — a project that deepened my appreciation for how different verification approaches complement each other. Alongside verification, I contributed to design integration, gaining a broader view of how individual IPs fit together into larger systems.

Two and a half years in, and promoted to Design Verification Engineer, I continue to work on the same kind of challenges that first drew me to this field: making sure that the silicon we design actually does what it’s supposed to do. I find deep satisfaction in closing coverage, catching edge cases, and handing off verified IP with confidence.

The opportunity to join ChipVerge is an extension of that same drive — working alongside talented engineers on demanding problems, contributing real value to clients, and continuing to grow in a field I genuinely care about.
